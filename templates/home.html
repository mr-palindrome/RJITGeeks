About-Us

RJIT Geeks, a Codechef Campus Chapter, has been established to create a coding environment and awareness in the campus, to help the students gain insight into the technical world necessary for their careers.

We organize different events, sessions, workshops, hackathons, classes many more to encourage the students and help them as their backbone.
